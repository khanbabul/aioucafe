CHOICE OF NAME

AIOU CAFÉ☕

Many peoples will think about that unique name I gave to my brand. 🤭
After a long research and asking for reviews from my friends and collegues, I came across to find a unique name. The psychology behind choosing the name AIOU Cafe is pretty simple, AIOU Clearly represents the university name "Allama Iqbal Open Univerity" for which I am providing services. And the word CAFÉ☕ is once my personal choice because you can say I'm a Coffee addict and taking Coffee always makes my brain work harder and be sharper.
Overall, I love it. The name is catchy, memorable, and directly related to my target audience (AIOU Students) while the word "CAFE" adds a friendly, approachable touch, suggesting a welcoming space for students to seek help. It's a great choice.
